About This Blog

Our Purpose

Green Perspectives exists to illuminate the interconnectedness between human wellbeing and planetary health. Founded by Nomalungelo Sithole, this blog combines scientific research with practical insights to demonstrate why environmental care isn't just optional - it's essential for our collective future.

Our Approach

  • Evidence-based environmental information
  • Solutions-focused content
  • Accessible explanations of complex issues
  • Diverse perspectives on sustainability

Why Environmental Care Matters

For Our Health

Clean air, safe drinking water, and nutritious food all depend on healthy ecosystems. Environmental degradation directly impacts human health through increased diseases, respiratory problems, and food insecurity.

For Our Economy

Natural resources form the foundation of global economies. Sustainable management ensures long-term prosperity rather than short-term gains followed by collapse.

For Future Generations

Every species lost, every degree of warming, and every polluted waterway represents stolen possibilities from those who will inherit the Earth after us.

For Social Justice

Environmental harm disproportionately affects marginalized communities. Addressing ecological issues is inseparable from pursuing equity and justice.
